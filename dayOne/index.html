4th of July Bakes Beans

It's great how a single meal can take you back dozens of years. This is one of those recipies that never seems to fail to impress.

I learned this recipe from the cousin of one of my college friends back in Nashville, Tenesee. We had an amazing 4th of July feast which included this recipe and some bratwurst like these Wisconsin Beer Brats

Prep Time:

10 min

Cook Time:

3+ hours

Servings:

12

Ingredients:

  • 1lb Bacon Chopped
  • 3 Cans Bush's Original Baked Beans
  • 1 Walla Walla Onion chopped
  • 3 Ground Garlic Cloves
  • 4 TBSP Mustard
  • 3 TBSP Molasses
  • 4 TBSP Brown Sugar

Directions:

Cook bacon until it is mostly cooked, then drain most of the grease and set aside.

Cook onion in remaining bacon grease.

Combine onions abd bacon, add garlic, and cook for 2-3 more minutes

Add beans and get to simmering temperature.

Add mustard until beans turn a similar color (nice yellow color)

Add brown sugard until sweetened to preference

Continue to simmer.

Expert Tips:

Burining off most of the liquid returns delicious hearty sticky beans

If the beans get too dry, add more beer!
